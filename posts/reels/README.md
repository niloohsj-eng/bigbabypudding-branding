# Reels — Video content + reel covers

Short-form video is the fastest organic growth tool on Instagram. Even one strong reel per week can outperform 10 static posts.

**Cadence:** **1x per week** to start. Scale up as you find what works.

## What goes here

- Reel video files (`.mp4` or `.mov`) — the actual videos
- Custom reel cover thumbnails (1080x1920) — so your reels look clean in the grid

## Reel ideas that work for food brands

### Process / ASMR
- 60-second timelapse of building one batch (layering wafers, slicing bananas, piping cream)
- Close-up spoon scoop, slow-motion (iPhone slow-mo @ 240fps, then slowed in app)
- Cup wrap being applied, smoothed onto the cup
- Wafer crunching close-up with audio

### Product
- 3-second "swipe through" of every flavor / variation
- The "first bite" reaction (you or a friend)
- "How it travels" — Deliveroo bag opens, cup pulled out, ready to eat

### Personality
- "POV: you ordered a Big Baby on a Friday night" — short skit
- "Things they don't tell you about banana pudding" — text overlay with 3–4 facts
- "What goes into one cup" — quick reveal of ingredients

### Trends
- Watch reels by similar brands (small bakeries, dessert shops, NYC banana pudding accounts) every week. When a trend works for them, adapt it to your product within 48 hours.

## Reel cover thumbnails

Each reel needs a **custom cover** so it doesn't break the grid. Build it like a poster:
- 1080x1920 (portrait)
- Cream or brown background per the brand
- Bradley Hand Bold for any text
- One subject (the food or character)
- Center the focal element so it looks good when cropped to a 1:1 grid thumbnail

When you create one, save it here as `reel_cover_<name>_<date>.png`.

## Caption rules for reels

Reels need more text-on-screen than caption — most viewers watch muted. Keep on-screen text:
- 3–5 words per beat
- High contrast (brown on cream or vice versa)
- One font (Bradley Hand Bold)

Caption itself: short, period at the end, one CTA at the end (*Tap the link in bio.* or *Order on Deliveroo.*)

## Sound

Use **trending audio** for reach. Open Reels, scroll for ~2 mins, save 5–10 trending sounds per week. Pick one that vibes with your video.

Avoid copyright-claimed music — use Instagram's library only.
