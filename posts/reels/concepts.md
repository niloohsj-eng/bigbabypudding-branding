# Reel concepts — story-driven ideas

Saved for next session. All character-driven with hook/beat/payoff. Aiming beyond the basic "spoon loop" — these have actual narrative.

## Character-driven

### 1. The Banana Auditions ⭐ (extends picky banana story)
- 3 bananas walk in (green / golden / overripe)
- Spotlight → green = ❌
- Spotlight → overripe = ❌
- Spotlight → golden = ✓, carried into cup
- Caption: *Three walked in. One got hired.*

### 2. The Hungry Big Baby Dive
- Cup of pudding centre frame
- Character appears, eyes wide
- Dives headfirst INTO the cup (splash)
- Pops up with cream moustache, happy
- Caption: *We get it.*

### 3. The Sneaky Banana
- Cup alone on counter
- Walking banana tiptoes in
- Looks left, looks right (no one)
- Eats entire mound in one bite
- Walks out with full belly + guilty smile
- Caption: *We don't blame them.*

## ASMR / satisfaction

### 4. The Layer Build
- Empty cup
- Banana slices fall in (small bounces)
- Cream pours from above
- Wafer crumbs sprinkle on top
- Final cup + sparkle
- Caption: *Step-by-step.*

### 5. The Perfect Scoop (loop)
- Slow-zoom on full cup
- Spoon enters in slow-mo
- Cream stretches as spoon lifts
- Suspended scoop, loops
- Caption: *On a loop.*

## Process / story

### 6. Cup Factory Line
- Empty cups roll across on a conveyor
- From above: banana drops → cream pours → wafer sprinkles → lid stamps "BIG BABY"
- Cup rolls off frame
- Caption: *Made one at a time.*

### 7. Notting Hill Delivery
- Pastel houses (from Notting Hill tile)
- Walking banana strolls past
- Door opens, hand reaches out with a cup
- Banana takes it, walks on happy
- Caption: *On the way to W8.*

## Quick punchy

### 8. The "Oh!" Moment
- Cup of pudding
- Spoon goes in, comes up with cream
- Cut to banana with "😋" face
- 2 seconds max
- Caption: *Same.*

## Notes on production

- Use existing visual language: cream/brown palette, hand-drawn cup style, Bradley Hand Bold
- Keep frame rate around 12fps for hand-drawn feel
- Always export both square (1080x1080) for posts AND vertical (1080x1920) for reels
- Length: 3-8 seconds for posts, 10-15 seconds for reels (looping the 5s base)
- Build with the same Python+PIL approach as spoon_loop.gif, then convert to MP4 via imageio_ffmpeg
